What is ERA5?

ERA5 is the fifth-generation reanalysis product produced by the Copernicus Climate Change Service (C3S) at ECMWF. It provides hourly estimates of a large number of atmospheric, land and oceanic climate variables covering the globe from 1940 onwards.

ERA5 combines vast amounts of historical observations with model data using advanced data assimilation techniques. This makes it an excellent resource for understanding climate variability and change, including wind patterns and extreme weather events.
